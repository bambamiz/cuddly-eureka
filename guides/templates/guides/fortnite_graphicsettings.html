Fortnite graphic settings

Follow along step by step or mix it up if you'd prefer.

1. Video settings

  • Resolution: By default your monitors resolution should already be set. If not, find out your monitors resolution by searching for the model number and set it to the correct resolution.
  • Frame rate limit: Ideally, you want to set your FPS to match or exceed your monitors refresh rate.
  • Nvidia reflex: If your system supports it I recommend turning it on.

1. Continued

  • Rendering mode: This varies from system to system. Keep in mind that DirectX12 and Performance are still in beta. The only way to know if they will work for you is to try it.

1. Continued

2. On screen display

  • I added this section incase you want to be able to view your fps and network stats. In the picture below, I outlined how the onscreen statistics look

2. continued

  • To turn on network stats (outlined in yellow in the picture above), go to settings, game ui and toggle on net debug stats.
  • To turn on the FPS counter (outlined in red in 2nd picture above), go to settings, video and toggle on show FPS.

There you have it, you're all set.

Summary

Settings are preference, usually when selecting your settings you are choosing to prioritize graphics quality or performance.

Personally I tend to play on low settings targeting at least 144fps, therefore my settings are performance based.
